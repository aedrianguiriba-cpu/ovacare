Grilled Bangus with Ensaladang Talong
A heart-healthy Filipino dish rich in omega-3 fatty acids and fiber that supports insulin balance and hormonal stability in PCOS.
Serving Size: 1 plate (~400 g)
Calories: ~420 kcal
Protein 34 g
Carbohydrates 30 g
Fat 20 g
Fiber 6 g
Ingredients
120 g bangus fillet
100 g eggplant
60 g tomato
30 g red onion
10 mL calamansi juice
5 mL olive oil
100 g cooked brown rice
Preparation
Grill bangus until internal temperature reaches 63°C. Grill eggplant until soft, peel and chop. Combine eggplant with tomato, onion, calamansi, and olive oil. Serve with measured brown rice.
Clinical Notes
Moderate carbohydrate load. Omega-3 supports anti-inflammatory response in PCOS.
